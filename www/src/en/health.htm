:: HEALTH ::

The SRC seeks to improve the health of the rural-youth by preventing sickness. Below you will find some more information about the techniques used to achieve this goal.
 

Water filtering

There is a plan to filter the water better so it will be drinkable.
 

Urinal

Sekyere momentarily does not have an adequate urinal. The SRC is building a new urinal.
 

Immunity shots

Immunity-days are held against many types of sicknesses (ie Polio and Measels).
 

Education

Research has shown that the rural adolescent does not receive enough education on their health and how to stay healthy.
They learn about foods, sicknesses and how to attend sick people. Special attention is given to STD's including HIV/AIDS.
 

Mobile clinics

Next to preventing sicknesses, the SRC also operates mobile clinics in the target communities.
